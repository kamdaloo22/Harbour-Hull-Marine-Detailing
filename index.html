Who We Are

Marine Care You Can Trust

Harbour & Hull was founded by two brothers. We started this business because we believe every boat owner deserves access to expert-level detailing done right, at a fair price, and because we genuinely love the water. Every job we take on is treated like it's our own boat: with patience, proper products, and real pride in the result.

  • Proudly serving British Columbia
  • No job too big, no vessel too pristine
